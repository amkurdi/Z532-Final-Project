What is Cricket?

Cricket was invented in the vast fields of England, supposedly by shepherds who herded their flock. Later on this game was shown benevolence by aristocrats, and now has the stature of being England's national game. After a century now, cricket stands in the international arena, with a place of its own. Cricket involves two teams with 11 players on each side. The captain who wins the toss decides whether his team bats or bowls first. If they bat first , their aim is to score a lot of runs and make sure the other team does not reach that score.

About our club

The club, which can be reached at bloomingtoncricket@gmail.com, strives to make cricket an easily-accessible sport by arranging cricket matches. Furthermore, IUBCC aims to provide a safe and fun environment for people (even non-students) to play cricket. Most matches are held on baseball fields throughout the city of Bloomington, and cricket equipment is provided to anyone who wishes to play. By promoting the sport of cricket, IUBCC hopes to encourage an active and healthy lifestyle.

The club holds several practice sessions and organizes and participates in different tournaments. IUBCC has a current member count of nearly 500 and is increasing gradually with more and more people joining the club. The club also organizes Hoosier cup, an annual cricket tournament where teams from across the state of Indiana participate. This year the Hoosier Cup will be September 19th at the Twin Lakes Sports Park in Bloomington.
